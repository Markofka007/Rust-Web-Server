Cat Facts

Back to Main Page

Cats are fascinating creatures. Here are some interesting cat facts:

  • Cats have a specialized collarbone that allows them to always land on their feet when they fall.
  • A cat's nose print is unique, similar to a human fingerprint.
  • Domestic cats can run up to 30 miles per hour!

Did you know that cats spend about two-thirds of their lives sleeping? That's a lot of catnaps!

Whether they're chasing laser pointers or curling up in a sunbeam, cats bring joy to our lives.
