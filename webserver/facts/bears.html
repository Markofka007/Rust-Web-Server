Bear Facts

Back to Main Page

Bears are powerful creatures. Here are some interesting bear facts:

  • The polar bear is the largest land carnivore and is perfectly adapted for life in the Arctic.
  • Black bears can be found in various habitats across North America, from forests to mountains.
  • Brown bears (also known as grizzlies) are skilled fishers and can catch salmon with ease.
  • Even though they look very cuddly, they can be very dangerous.

Did you know that bears hibernate during winter? They enter a deep sleep and rely on stored fat for survival.

Whether they're foraging for berries or exploring their vast territories, bears command respect in the wild.
